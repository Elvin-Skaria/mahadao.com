Technology
ARTH is backed by a stable basket of assets calculated via a proprietary index called the “Global Measurement Unit” which informs the ARTH target price. Additional robust strategies such as seigniorage shares, automatic rebalancing and trusted oracle price feeds also assist to further stabilise the price of ARTH.
